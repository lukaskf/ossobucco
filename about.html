Minneapolis, MN Who Are We

Founded January 2019, osso bucco aims to celebrate the lives we live through creativity, vibrance, energy, and radiance. Painting, singing, skateboarding, sculpting, dancing, screaming, meditating, eating, chopping wood and carrying water. Love everyone and stay true.

Local artists in the Twin Cities weave human vitality into the design of our goods. Join us this Spring and celebrate at our launch event - T.B.D. - updates and news can be found at our instagram @ossobucco_official.
