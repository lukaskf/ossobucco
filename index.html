Minneapolis, MN About Us

Founded January 2019, osso bucco is a clothing and skateboard company celebrating vibrance and creativity. Our designs are made in collaboration with local Twin Cities artists, who bring their style and influence into our products. We strive to produce goods to inspire and excite members of our community.

Join us this Spring and celebrate at our launch event - T.B.D. - updates and news can be found at our instagram. Our vision is to host quarterly events that bring together the Minneapolis community to enjoy wearable art, music, skateboarding, and good people.
